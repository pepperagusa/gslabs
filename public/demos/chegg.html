Book Condition

We only purchase books in Very Good condition or better. Books in this condition do not have the following:

  • Broken spine or binding
  • Missing, torn or loose pages
  • Excessive writing, marking or highlighting
  • Stains or water damage
  • Burns or rips
  • Any other significant damage
  • Chegg can only purchase physical books and does not purchase eTextbooks

Quick Tips:

  • The quote expires in 7 days
  • You can only sell 10 of the same ISBN and a total of 40 books per customer/per month
  • Binder, teacher and international editions are also ineligible

*Please note: We cannot return books that don’t meet our condition requirements. This evaluation may take up to 5 business days. Please examine your books carefully before sending them to Chegg for purchase.
